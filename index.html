How to Play

  • Welcome to Guess My Number! Try to guess the correct number between 1 and 20.
  • Enter your guess in the input field and click the "Check!" button to see if you're correct.
  • You start with a score of 20, and each incorrect guess will decrease your score.
  • The game will provide feedback on whether your guess is too high or too low.
  • If you guess the correct number, you win the game!
  • Your highscore will be updated if you beat your previous best.
  • Click the "Again!" button to start a new game and reset the score.
  • Feel free to challenge yourself and see how quickly you can guess the correct number with the highest score possible.
